4.Burpees

DO Squat down low to the ground, place your hands out in front of you and jump your legs out behind you so you are in a push up position. Quickly jump your feet back up to your hands again, returning to the initial position. Stand upright and jump up into the air before repeating the movement.

DONT Let yourself slack off. Burpees are hard but they are in these circuits for a reason – they are an all over body resistance exercise and also incredible cardio, meaning they will burn serious amounts of glycogen, fat and calories. Give them everything you’ve got!
