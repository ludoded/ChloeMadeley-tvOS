3. Bicycles

DO Lie on your back and bend your knees. Lift your upper body towards your knees, bringing your right elbow across to your left knee as you do, so you perform a sort of diagonal crunch. Staying in the crunch position, then bring your left elbow across to your right knee - and so on and so forth.

DONT Let yourself drop out of the crunch position. Once you have performed that first bicycle, stay in the v position as you switch elbows and direction. Make sure the strain is on your core throughout.
