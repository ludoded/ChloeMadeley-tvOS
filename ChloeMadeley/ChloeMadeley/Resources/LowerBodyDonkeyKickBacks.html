3. Donkey Kick Backs

DO Get on all fours and bring your right knee into your chest, then slowly extend the leg behind yourself and up into the air, pointing your toe at your leg’s vertical peak. Repeat this movement for half the time required, and then switch legs.

DONT Get excited and go too fast. This is one of those weird exercises that you don’t really feel until the next day. To get the full benefit of the exercise, you need to really think about the movement and muscles required, and perform methodically.
