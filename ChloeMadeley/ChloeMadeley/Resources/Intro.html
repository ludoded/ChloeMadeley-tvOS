Welcome

Hi I’m Chloe Madeley. Welcome to my 15-minute Fat Loss Circuits.

I’m going to be your personal trainer. All I ask is that you really push yourself for 15 minutes every single day.

I have designed four fat loss circuits for you guys.

Each one targets a different area: your upper body, mid-section, lower body and finally an all over cardiovascular serious fat-burning circuit.

In every circuit there are four exercises. I want you to perform them for one minute each, back to back.

Then rest for one minute. Repeat the circuit twice more.

That brings you to a grand total of 15 minutes, which really is all you need when it comes to this kind of high-intensity training, fat-burning exercises.

So, when you’re ready…let’s go!

Stretch and warm up

Before your perform each circuit, remember that you must ALWAYS warm up and warm down so you avoid injury. I do this every day without fail.

Warm ups should be gentle but slightly dynamic, similar to the exercises themselves, but with a slow and calm performance, for 8-10 seconds.

Warm downs should be static. You want to stretch out each muscle singularly and hold the stretch, again for 8-10 seconds.

Exercise safely

While performing these circuits you should be out of breath, sweating and or in muscular discomfort.

However, if you feel something pull sharply, hear any strange internal noises, vomit, find that any of your peripheral senses are distorted, and or feel generally unwell, you must stop immediately.

Please also bear in mind that when you are ill, your heart becomes enlarged, and you are at a greater risk of heart attacks during exercise.
