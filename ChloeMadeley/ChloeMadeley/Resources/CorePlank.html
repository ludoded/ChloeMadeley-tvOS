2. Plank

DO Lie on your stomach and then elevate your body using the backs of your forearms and balls of your feet. Make sure your bum isn’t elevated in the air or that your hips aren’t lowered to the ground. This move is called The Plank for a reason – be strong, solid and straight – think like a plank!

DONT Let your back dip or curve down - you want your bum in line with the rest of your body. Don’t elevate your bum either. You want to feel the strain predominantly in your core.
