2. Walkout Push Ups

DO Place your hands on the ground in front of you and walk your body out so you are in a push up position. Keeping your body straight and solid like you would in a plank, lower your nose to the ground and then push up, returning to the initial position.

DONT Drop your hips low to the ground OR elevate your hips into the air. Think of the initial push up position similarly to a plank position, your body needs to be straight and solid, so that your upper body really feels the strain of the push up movement itself.
