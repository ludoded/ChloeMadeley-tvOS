<h2 class="p1"><span style="color: #ff00ff;">2. Walking Lunges</span></h2>

<p class="p2"><span style="color: #ff00ff;"><strong><span class="s1">DO </span></strong></span>Place your hands on your hips, take a big step forward and start to get down on bended knee. When your back knee almost touches the floor, stand back up again and repeat the movement with the opposite leg.</p>

<p class="p2"><span style="color: #ff00ff;"><strong><span class="s1">DONT </span></strong></span>Let your front knee extend beyond your front toe, or fall short of it. You want your knee to be directly above your front toe.</p>
