1. Mountain Climbers

DO Place your hands on the ground in front of you and walk your body out so you are in a push up position. Keeping your body straight and solid like you would in a plank, bring 1 knee up to your chest and then rapidly switch knees. You’re essentially doing high-knees in a horizontal position.

DONT Drop your hips low to the ground OR elevate your hips into the air. Think of the initial push up position similarly to a plank position, your body needs to be straight and solid, so that your upper body really feels the strain of the push up movement itself.
