4. Leg Plunges

DO Lie on your back. Keeping your legs straight, cross your ankles. Swing your legs upward into a vertical position, raising your hips a little at their peak, then lower your legs back down.

DONT Concern yourself with the speed at which you are performing the exercise. You will feel a real strain in your lower abdominals as your bring your legs back down, this is the entire point of the exercise. If you try to then rush through them, you will not get the full benefit of the exercise.
